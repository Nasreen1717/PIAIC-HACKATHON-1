### What is Vision-Language-Action (VLA)?

**Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** is an integrated framework that combines three powerful capabilities:

1. **Vision** - Deep learning models for scene understanding, object detection, and spatial reasoning
2. **Language** - Large Language Models (LLMs) for natural language understanding and reasoning
3. **Action** - Robotic control systems that translate understanding into precise motor commands

Together, these create **embodied AI agents** that can:
- Understand spoken and written instructions from humans
- Perceive and comprehend their environment
- Reason about complex tasks and sub-tasks
- Execute precise, coordinated robotic actions
- Learn from experience and adapt to new scenarios
